About Us

The Kozhikkadu Bhagavathy Temple at Kozhikkadu near Thiruvazhiyad in Nemmara, managed by the Cochin Devaswom Board, is a beloved spiritual center for the local community. Revered as the Kuladevata (family deity) for many, the temple is known for the swift blessings of Devi. Surrounded by lush paddy fields and the shade of a majestic Banyan tree, the temple offers a peaceful and uplifting atmosphere. Daily rituals, performed by a dedicated priest, ensure a deeply spiritual experience for all who visit.
